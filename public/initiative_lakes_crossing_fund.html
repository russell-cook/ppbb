Cost Analysis


This is a new program requiring just over $3.0 million in new funding during the 2014-15 biennium.

This initiative requires just over $3.0 million in Tobacco funding during the 2014-15 biennium. The use of General Funds was also considered as an alternative funding approach.

Twenty new staff would be needed to support this expansion (Table A). Lake’s Crossing has already begun the process to hire this staff. A Board Certified Forensic Psychologist started September 1, 2013. A nurse has also been hired. These individuals are in other vacant position control numbers until Lake’s Crossings positions become available October 1. All position classifications have recruitment lists and interviews are being scheduled. It is anticipated that the psychiatrist position will present challenges in terms of hiring, but recruitment has begun.
