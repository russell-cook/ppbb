Percent of Clients Judged Competent at Discharge

Note: The declining number of competent defendants can probably be accounted for by two factors. The first is the persisting increase in the acuity of clients both medically and psychiatrically. These clients are observed to be more and more difficult to treat and restore due damaging drug use and the erratic treatment course for many where they are on and off psychiatric medications. A second reason is that the courts are less willing to allow involuntary medication for treatment to competency and these clients are deemed incompetent because that facility is unable to deliver treatment to them. In this case the only option is transfer into the civil system.
