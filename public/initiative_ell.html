Initiative: English Language Learners

Summary

Details

The Department of Education is proposing the investment of $50 million across the State to improve literacy of children who do not speak English as their primary language. This initiative will focus efforts on children attending Nevada’s public schools in kindergarten, first and second grades.

Justification/Reason
Details

It is estimated that 200,000 children in Nevada’s schools (18%) do not speak English as their primary language. It is further estimated that over 30% of these children will fail to read at grade level by the third grade without further assistance. Numerous studies indicate that reading at grade level by the third grade is a key predictor of ultimate graduation success.

Lead Agency: Department of Education
Secondary Agencies: None
