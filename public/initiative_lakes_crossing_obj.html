Benchmark Details: Poor Mental Health

What this Benchmark is Measuring?

This benchmark measures the number of days in the previous month that an individual could not perform work or household tasks due to mental illness.

Why this benchmark is an important measure of Government performance:

Access to appropriate mental health services helps stabilize individuals, families, and communities.

Data Source:

United Health Foundation, America's Health Rankings

Benchmark Details: Reduce Repeat Criminal Activity

What this Benchmark is Measuring?

This benchmark measures the success of correctional efforts to reduce recidivism. For adults, Nevada Parole and Probation measures the percent of offenders revoked.

Why this benchmark is an important measure of Government performance:

This measures the effectiveness of efforts to improve the probability that offenders successfully re-enter society.
Adults: Nevada Offender Tracking Information System (OTIS)
Youth Parole: UNITY System

Data Source:

Adults: Nevada Offender Tracking Information System - OTIS
Youth Parole: UNITY System
